Our Products — Helping you unlock financial opportunities

GroSave
Murabaha Inventory Financing

Helping restaurants, pharmacies, groceries, and cooperatives access inventory through ethical, asset-backed financing.

What it covers: Bulk inventory purchase, supplier onboarding, logistics support and repayment schedules tailored to trade cycles.

Structure: Murabaha purchase — HalalCredit buys inventory and sells to the client on a disclosed profit margin and schedule (no interest).

Ideal for: Restaurants, grocery stores, pharmacies, cooperatives.

Akkommodate
Ijarah Rental Financing

Supporting employees and professionals with flexible, Shariah-compliant rental financing. 50% equity contribution, 50% financed by HalalCredit, repaid in instalments.

How it works: Client contributes equity. HalalCredit purchases the asset and leases it to the client under an Ijarah contract. Client may acquire ownership later.

Benefits: Predictable rentals, clear exit/buyout options, structured documentation.

HalalCredit Card
0% Markup Consumer Financing

Creditworthy business owners and professionals enjoy 0% markup financing with only a fixed annual membership fee.

Perks: 0% mark-up financing on approved purchases, quick approvals, mobile account management.

Eligibility: Small business owners and professionals meeting minimum income criteria.
